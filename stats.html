Def. Statistics

The table broadly shows that males have a higher percentage of having ADHD than females, and that in males, 15.4% of all men will be diagnosed with ADHD, compared to 6.8% of all women. Generally, men are about 3 times more likely to have this disorder than women.

The bottom part of the above image affirms the first point, and it also shows that in households where English is the main language, children are more than four times likely to get ADHD than in households where English is not the first language.

1 in 6 Australians means theres around 3.3 million Australians suffering from anxiety at any given time. Out of those people, 19% are separated, while 13% of them are in a relationship. Australia actually has a lower rate of anxiety than Americans; 14.4% in Australia, compared to 20% in Australia. Women are more likely to suffer from anxiety than men: 18% of women do, whereas 11% of men suffer from anxiety.

In terms of depression and suicide rates, men aged 85 years or more had a high suicide rate of 37.6% per 100,000. Suicide rates corresponds to the depression rates, as around 90% of people who commit suicide do so because of depression. 85 year old or more aged men commit suicide as they lose their spouses, a sense of fulfilment and purpose from having a job, losing friends, and experiencing social isolation.
